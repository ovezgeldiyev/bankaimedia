Frequently Asked Questions

How work intensive will it be on my end?

We handle all aspects of the process. All we need from you is any relevant brand resources we might need upfront, and your time for a short zoom meeting every month.

How do I know if Bankai Media has my best interests?

We tailor-make each aspect of ZenFlow Pro for your specific brand. We treat each clients brand like if it was our own. Our client relationships are just as important to us as bringing fabulous results.

How long will it be until I see a good return on my investment?

As part of the process detailed above, increased metrics will start occurring within the second month. This is still a month of detailed testing and optimization however, so growth will only go up from there.

How will you keep in contact with me? What kind of communication should I expect?

Our default line of communication occurs through a monthly zoom meeting. You can email us at any time in between then. If you would like a different schedule for meetings, we would be happy to accommodate a more frequent schedule, or even a lengthier interval.

I only recently started my
E-Commerce business. Do you cater to small businesses?

Yes we do. If you sell online, you are in our specialty. While we may discuss tweaking a few elements of the implemented strategy for your brand, we are thrilled at the opportunity to help grow a new brand from the ground up.

What languages will Bankai Media operate in for clients?

While currently operating only in English for clients, we are actively working on expanding our communication and fluency for the Spanish language.

How involved can I be working with Bankai Media?

For the eager entrepreneurs, or the sit-back and manage type, you can be as involved or laid-back as you desire. Collaboration will never be turned down as we understand the success you strive for.
